Mental Health Quiz

Please honestly answer the questions below!

Question 1: Have you felt hopeless in the past 90 days?
Question 2: Do you have little interest in activities you used to enjoy?
Question 3: Have you having trouble falling or staying sleep, or sleeping too much?
Question 4: Do you feel tired or have little energy?
Question 5: Do you have poor appetite or have you been overeating in the past 30 days?
Question 6: Do you have trouble concentrating on tasks and schoolwork?
Question 7: Do you have thoughts that you would be better off dead, or of hurting yourself?
Question 8: Have you felt bad about yourself or that you have let your friends and family down?
Question 9: Have you been moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed -or the opposite - being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual?
Question 10: If you checked off any problems, has it been difficult for you at work, home, or with other people?
